# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 50,000+ sales transactions across multiple coffee shop locations. Features automated data cleaning via Power Query, complex data modeling with Pivot Tables, and dynamic visualizations to identify peak sales hours and product performance.
## Dataset Used 
- <a href= "https://github.com/kedarjadhav365-cell/Coffee-Shop-Sales-Performance-Dashboard/blob/main/Coffee_shop_sales_analysis_project.xlsx"> Dataset </a>
## Business Question Answered 

**What are the peak transaction hours, and how should staffing be adjusted to minimize wait times?**

**Which product categories drive the highest sales volume versus the highest revenue?**

**How do sales patterns differ across the Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, and Lower Manhattan locations?**

**What is the month-over-month trend in quantity sold, and how can we optimize stock levels?**

**What is the distribution of product price points, and how does it correlate with transaction frequency?**

##Key Insights
1.**The Morning Spike**: Sales triple between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM. This is the "Golden Window" for maximum staffing.
2.**Top Sellers**: Coffee and Tea drive the most volume, while Bakery items (Scones/Biscotti) are the best revenue-boosting "add-ons."
3.**Location Leade**r: Astoria is the most consistent store; Lower Manhattan spikes specifically during office commute hours.
4**.Price Sweet Spot**: Most customers prefer products priced between $2.50 and $4.00, showing high sensitivity to mid-range pricing.
5.**Growth Trend**: Average daily sales increased in February, indicating rising customer loyalty despite fewer calendar days.
6.**PM Opportunity**: Sales dip after 2:00 PM, revealing a perfect chance for "Afternoon Happy Hour" promotions.

##Conclusion

This project demonstrates the power of transforming 50,000+ rows of raw data into actionable business strategy. By using Power Query for ETL and Pivot Tables for analysis, I identified clear operational bottlenecks and revenue-growth opportunities.
The resulting dashboard provides a roadmap to optimize staffing, improve inventory, and increase profitability across multiple retail locations.
